Hi, I'm the artist behind Chandellure

Chandellure started as a passion project in a tiny home studio and grew into something I never imagined — a small business that lets me share my love of colour, texture, and craftsmanship with the world.

I work with high-quality, non-toxic epoxy resin and source natural inclusions like dried flowers, shells, and pigments from ethical suppliers. Each piece takes anywhere from one to five days to complete, from pouring to final polish.

No two pieces are ever the same, and that's exactly the point. You'll own something truly unique.

— Chandellure ✦
